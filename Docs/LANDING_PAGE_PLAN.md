## üìù Content Writing Tips

### Tone of Voice

-  **Professional yet approachable** - Not overly corporate
-  **Confident** - We solve real problems
-  **Educational** - Help users understand value
-  **Inspiring** - Motivate students to succeed

### Writing Guidelines

-  Use **active voice** ("AI creates your study plan" vs. "Study plan is created
   by AI")
-  **Short sentences** - 15-20 words max
-  **Benefit-focused** - What's in it for them?
-  **Specific numbers** - "500 questions" not "lots of questions"
-  **Action verbs** - Start, Create, Track, Generate, Master
-  **Avoid jargon** - Use simple language

### Example Good vs. Bad Copy

```
‚ùå Bad: "Our platform utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms
to optimize your learning trajectory."

‚úÖ Good: "AI creates a study plan based on your exam date that adapts
```
